About

Hello!

My name is Spencer Riner. I'm 24 and attending UHCL with a major in Computer Information Systems. My focus at this time is Object Oriented Programming, though I have a wide variety of interests beyond that. I like to learn new things, like how to make these popup boxes, and hope to keep pushing myself both technically and creatively in the future.

Programming

I've studied and practiced object oriented programming since my first year of college in 2008. I started with C++, and later moved to Java. Later this year I plan on expanding into C# to work on creating a video game. I really enjoy programming because of its predictable, logical nature; computers by nature are incredibly obedient. Because of this property, they are an excellent tool to use to solve and understand problems, and make life a bit more convenient at times.

Web Development

I was lucky to grow up in a time where the Internet was just getting off its feet and becoming a common utility. Because of this, I was able to start making my own websites at a very young age with the advent of free hosting sites like Geocities and Angelfire. I feel thankful for that, since it made learning serious web development concepts (including building this very website) much more intuitive for me. As I've cycled through iterations of my own personal website, I've learned about how to use and manipulate CSS and LESS, Javascript and jQuery, as well as Github for hosting and version control.

Mobile/Responsive

I use Bootstrap to ensure websites I'm working on are easy to view on any type of device. Try viewing this site on your tablet or mobile phone. I am currently in the process of learning Objective C to write iPhone and iPad apps.

Music

I love music. I play guitar, electric bass and sing occasionally. I have been making my own music under the pseudonym Fredman Fredd for almost 3 years; it's been a very satisfying ride. You can find music I've made on SoundCloud and BandCamp.
