about me

My name is Spencer Riner, and I am a student at University of Houston Clear Lake. I'll graduate in May of 2016 with a B.S. in Computer Information Systems. I've always had a penchant for computers, and anything with moving parts; I took apart my first guitar promptly, to my dad's dismay. I spent a few years working retail at various stores you have probably heard of, and still work about one day a week while going to school.

I have a music project called Fredman Fredd that takes up any of my time not spent studying or working on side projects. We have a Bandcamp, Soundcloud, and Facebook page. I work alongside my good friends Jay and Kris to try and make songs that we enjoy. It's been good so far.
